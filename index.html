Famous places In India

Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the land of royals, offers a vibrant tapestry of culture, history, and natural beauty

Ladakh

Ladakh, the land of high passes, is a stunning region known for its breathtaking landscape.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is built of white marble that reflects hues according to the intensity of the sunlight or the moonlight.

Jaipur

Jaipur is a major hub for arts and crafts. It has many traditional shops selling antiques, jewellery.

Goa

Golden beaches and rich culture, Goa is a lively coastal state in India.

Shimla

Shimla is home to many temples and holy shrines.

Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer is also known for its leather messenger bags, made from wild camels native to the area.

Udaipur

Udaipur, known as the "Lake City," is famous for its beautiful lakes

Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Banaras, is one of the oldest living cities in the world
